Dishcover

I recently had the opportunity to work on an exciting Android-based food recommendation system called "Dishcover" as part of the Bangkit Academy 2023 Batch 2 program. This project involved implementing machine learning models to classify food ingredients captured by the device's camera, and then providing personalized recipe recommendations based on the recognized ingredients. Fire Alarm Monitoring System

Another project I have been involved in is the "Fire Alarm Monitoring System", an Android-based IoT solution designed to provide comprehensive monitoring and alert capabilities for potential fire hazards. This system utilizes various sensors to continuously monitor the conditions within a designated space, and can automatically trigger an alarm in the event of a fire. Furthermore, the system is also capable of detecting the presence and status of individuals within the monitored area, thereby facilitating more efficient evacuation procedures in emergency situations. By combining my expertise in mobile application development, IoT system design, and sensor integration, I have created a practical solution that addresses critical safety concerns and enhances emergency response capabilities.
